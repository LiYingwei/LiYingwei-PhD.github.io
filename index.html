I am a 2nd-year PhD student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins Universit, advised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Dr. Alan Yuille. I am a member of Computational Cognition, Vision, and Learning.

I obtained B.S. in Computer Science at Fudan University in 2018. I also speet time in NTU, TuSimple and ByteDance AI Lab.
